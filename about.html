About Us

When the corona pandemic hit everything changed we were all hit with a wave of hard economic times,with the random job losses and shortage in cashflow circulation it came to a point where every shilling mattered. But still even as wait for the economic environment to get better what if we came up with a way to help the common mwananchi save as little as a bob in their daily expenditure

What We do


OkoaBob is a product cost comparison app, that takes in a specific product then compares the prices of the product in a number of common retail supermarket stores then it lets the user know at which store is he/she most likely to spend the least thus enabling them to save a shilling(bob) or more.

OKoa bob website will allow the user login into the website and access the page to enable them select their product and get different retail prices in different retail stores thereafter he or she can make an informed choice on where to do their shopping
