About OkoaBob

OkoaBob is a product cost comparison app, that takes in a specific product then compares the prices of the product in a number of common retail supermarket stores then it lets the user know at which store is he/she most likely to spend the least thus enabling them to save a shilling(bob) or more.At the click of a button
